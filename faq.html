FAQ

What does raw mean?

Raw honey is never overheated and not ultra-filtered, so it keeps more of its natural flavor, aroma, and character.

Why does honey vary by season?

Each harvest reflects what the bees were visiting. Different blooms, weather, and timing naturally change color, texture, and taste.

Why did my honey crystallize?

Crystallization is natural in real honey. Some people prefer the thick consistency, but if you want to soften your honey, warm it gently around 95–100°F. Avoid microwaving or boiling.

How should I store honey?

Keep it sealed at room temperature in a dry spot out of direct sun. With proper storage, honey keeps very well.
