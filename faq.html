FAQ

What does “raw honey” mean?

Raw honey is never overheated and not ultra-filtered, so it keeps more of its natural flavor, aroma, and character.

Why does honey taste a little different through the year?

Honey reflects what the bees forage each season. Weather, bloom timing, and local flowers all shape color and flavor.

Why did my honey crystallize?

Crystallization is natural and a sign of real honey. Many people love the thick consistency, but if you want to soften your honey, warm it gently around 95–100°F. Do not microwave and do not boil.

How should I store honey?

Keep it sealed at room temperature in a dry place. Honey lasts a very long time when stored well.
