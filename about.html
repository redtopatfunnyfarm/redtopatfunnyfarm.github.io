Meet the Maker

Welcome to Funny Farm — my little corner of Western New York where I keep bees, grow peppers, and make small-batch goods I’m proud to share.

My beekeeping journey started years ago in Corfu, New York, and it’s grown into a farm-made rhythm I love: tending hives, watching the blooms change, and bottling honey that tastes like the season.

I believe local bees matter — for the health of our land, our gardens, and our food. Raw honey is one of nature’s simplest gifts, and I do my best to keep it honest: small harvests, careful handling, and never overheated.

How I work

Everything I make is small batch and seasonal by nature. I keep it personal so every jar and bottle stays true to the farm.

From the farm & the flowers

Every harvest has its own floral signature. The blooms change, the nectar changes, and the honey changes — that’s the magic.
