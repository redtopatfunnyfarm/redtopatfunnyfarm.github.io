Meet the Maker

I’m so glad you’re here. Funny Farm is a small farmstead shop rooted in care, creativity, and local connection.

A woman-owned, single-producer farmstead shop

This is truly hands-on work from one maker. I keep things small so I can stay close to quality, keep every batch personal, and share food and body-care goods I feel proud to put my name on.

How I make things at Funny Farm

I raise bees, grow peppers, forage herbs, and craft products in small batches with love and intention. If I ever need help, I partner only with trusted makers who respect the same standards for clean ingredients and careful process.

What you’ll find here

You’ll find pure raw honey, farm-to-bottle hot sauce, wildcrafted creations, and natural health & beauty essentials designed for everyday use.
