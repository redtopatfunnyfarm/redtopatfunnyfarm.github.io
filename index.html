Why Our Honey

  • Raw & unfiltered — never overheated.

From the Farm, Not a Factory

Funny Farm is woman-owned, single-producer, and truly small batch. Every jar is harvested, bottled, and labeled by hand right here at home.

Our bees follow the rhythm of Western New York seasons, so flavor and color naturally shift through the blooms. That local character is exactly what makes each harvest special.
